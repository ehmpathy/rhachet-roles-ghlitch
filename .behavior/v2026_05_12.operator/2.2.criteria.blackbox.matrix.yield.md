## gaps identified

### gap.1 = query.cloudwatch.* env includes test?

the blackbox criteria shows env as "prep or prod" but use.rds.capacity shows "test, prep, prod". clarify whether cloudwatch queries support test env.

**recommendation**: cloudwatch queries likely prod/prep only (no test cloudwatch). document explicitly.

### gap.2 = invoke.vital in observer but can take action

invoke.vital is in observer role but some vitals may take corrective action. the blackbox criteria says "vitals that act should be commands" — verify this boundary is enforced in implementation.

**recommendation**: vitals must be read-only. any vital that takes action should be refactored to invoke.command.
